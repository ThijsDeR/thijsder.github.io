About the Game

Prepare for an epic battle against relentless viruses in "Idle Server Defender," the most thrilling server-themed Idle Tower Defense mobile game! Immerse yourself in a world where your strategic skills are put to the ultimate test as you defend your server from waves of digital threats.

Key Features

  • Endless upgrades for your server's stats.
  • Manage resources to enhance your server.
  • Utilize element slots for a strategic advantage.
